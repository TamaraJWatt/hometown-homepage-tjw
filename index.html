"Gateway to the North"

Visit EDMONTON, Alberta, Canada

Edmonton is the most Northerly city with a population over a million people in North America

Highlights of Edmonton:

Attend a Festival!

Edmonton has over 100 festivals year round. Themes include: cultural, food, theatre, amusement, & music festivals.

West Edmonton Mall

Something for everyone...shopping, swimming, skating, golfing, gambling, gokarting, cinema, dinner theatre or visit the amusement park.

The Canadian Rocky Mountains

Jasper & Banff National Parks. A four hour drive west, a must see for all visitors.
