The Canadian Rocky Mountains

Fast Facts

Capital of the province of Alberta, Canada

Established in 1795 (Fort Edmonton) as a trading post

The River Valley is 22x the size of NY's Central Park and the largest stretch of urban parkland in North America with 275km of paved and 460km of unpaved trails.

Home of the Edmonton Oilers (NHL Hockey), 5 time Stanely Cup Champions

The Aurora Borealis (Northern Lights) can be seen here.

Has the best bakery in Canada, The Dutchess Bake Shop

One of Canada's sunniest cities with over 2300 bright sunny hours per year.
